NetSuite Senior Consultant (Technical)

Position:

Senior Consultant

Experience:

5-8 Years

Key Responsibilities:
  • Handling client requirement gathering sessions along with Functional consultant.
  • Converting client requirements into scalable and efficient technical solutions.
  • Developing Custom functionalities using NetSuite’s development platform.
  • Learning ETL tools and implementing integrations between NetSuite and other ERP/CRM/HRM systems.
  • Knowledge in NetSuite Bundle and Bundle installations.
  • Proficient at NetSuite Coding using SuiteScript, SuiteCloud, SuiteFlow, SuiteBuilder, SuiteTalk.
  • Customize NetSuite fields, page layouts, record types, reports and dashboards.
  • Knowledge of Dell Boomi, Workato, and/or Celigo is a plus.
  • Testing newly developed functionalities & annual releases.
  • Technical documentation of the new functionalities.
  • Working with clients to resolve production issues.
  • Experience with REST and SOAP Services.
Qualifications
Required:
  • A Bachelor’s degree in CS or equivalent.
  • NetSuite Suitescript Development experience is mandatory.
  • 4+ years of experience using NetSuite’s JavaScript API and SuiteCloud platforms (Suitescript 1.0 & 2.0, SuiteTalk, and SuiteFlow).
  • Experience with and understanding of operational processes and requirements in the ERP system.
  • Good understanding of Order to Cash, Order to Pay, Record to Report process, and experience developing solutions in NetSuite.
  • Experience defining systems strategy, developing systems requirements, designing and prototyping, testing, and training, defining support procedures, and implementing multiple business solutions under tight time constraints.
Preferred:
  • Familiarity with SuiteCloud developer/engineer tools
  • Experience with Agile, Waterfall, and SDLC methodologies
  • Strong familiarity with document repositories
  • Knowledge of ETL tools
