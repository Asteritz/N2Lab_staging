Client Overview

Our Flash Storage Customer specialize in the development of all-flash data storage hardware and software products. With a global presence and multiple subsidiaries, they faced significant challenges related to revenue recognition and complex tax determinations. This case study explores how our expert team helped our customer overcome these challenges and achieve remarkable results.

Challenges

1. Revenue Recognition Across Subsidiaries

The Flash Storage Company operated with a complex organizational structure, with assets owned by a central subsidiary while needing to book revenue in various local subsidiaries. This complex structure made it challenging to accurately recognize and allocate revenue, leading to potential financial discrepancies.

2. Complex Tax Determination

Navigating complex tax regulations across different regions and subsidiaries posed a significant challenge for them. Manual tax determination processes were not only time-consuming but also prone to errors, potentially resulting in non-compliance and financial penalties.

What N2 Lab Proposed?

To address these challenges, The Flash Storage Company partnered with our expert team to implement the following solutions:

Cross-Subsidiary Fulfillment Implementation

N2 Lab facilitated the implementation of cross-subsidiary fulfillment, enabling the Flash Storage Company to efficiently manage revenue recognition across various subsidiaries. This solution streamlined the Order-to-Cash (O2C) process and ensured accurate revenue allocation.

Salesforce (SFDC) Enhancement

Our expert team also helped The Flash Storage Company to enhance their Salesforce (SFDC) system. By adding custom business rules to determine subsidiary relationships, they ensured that revenue was correctly attributed to the appropriate subsidiary, simplifying their revenue recognition process.

Results

The implementation of our solutions brought about significant improvements for Flash Storage Company:

Streamlined O2C Cross-Subsidiary Fulfillment

Automated Tax Determination and Invoice Printout

Increased Reporting Accuracy

Conclusion

By leveraging NetSuite's robust ERP capabilities, the Flash Storage Company successfully addressed their complex revenue recognition and tax determination challenges. This case study demonstrates how a tailored N2 Lab solution can empower organizations to streamline their financial processes, improve reporting accuracy, and achieve operational excellence. The Flash Storage Company's partnership with N2 Lab stands as a testament to the transformative impact of cutting-edge ERP solutions in the tech industry.
