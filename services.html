N2 Lab has a team of highly competent EDI experts that deliver integration solutions with careful integration considerations in a context-sensitive integration.

Features

  • Enterprise partnered integration with Boomi, Celigo and Workato.
Benefits
  • Improve performances with live data access and exchanges.

  • Reduce costs with less manual processing and potential costly rework due to errors.

Optimizing Procure to Pay Processes

Optimizing the Procure-to-Pay (P2P) process in NetSuite involves streamlining vendor onboarding and approvals, implementing 2-3 way matching controls, automating vendor bill acknowledgments, and establishing a Purchasing Approval Matrix for efficient authorization. These steps enhance transparency, accuracy, and speed in the P2P cycle. With N2 Lab's expertise, clients can trust in the right team to navigate these optimizations effectively.

Features
  • Streamlined procurement processes which are scalable, making it possible for you to deal with more customers and more complicated tasks.
  • Automated procurement for faster cycles and few payments delays.
  • Purchase approvals with 2/3 way matching.
Benefits
  • Reduces procurement errors, for example, overpay or overorder.
  • Improve efficiency and reduce processing cost with higher speed and accuracy.
  • Mitigate non-compliance with approval workflow, combined with documentation and audit trails.

Multibook Accounting

Implement NetSuite Multi-Book Accounting features which provides the ability to maintain multiple sets of accounting records based on a single set of real-time financial transactions; N2 Lab possesses a track record of successfully implementing Multi-Book solution to our clients through a team of Oracle NetSuite Authorised-Multi-Book consultants.

Features
  • Maintain a single set of financial transactions in different time zones, currencies and/or methods.
  • Maintain consistency, accuracy, and legality in financial reporting in all operating countries.
  • Make the necessary customizations in your accounting processes with ongoing compliance.
  • Smooth transition from old to new revenue principles.
Benefits
  • Ensure ongoing compliance with each set of standards, such as Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP) or local statutory requirements when operating in multiple countries.
  • Streamline communications and optimize business processes among various stakeholders.
  • Optimize financial close process and provide real-time visibility for any book, anytime.
