Client Overview

Our Retail Technology Customer is the leading health and wellness platform, selling prescription and over-the-counter drugs online, as well as personal care products. With significantly high subscriber and revenue grow, they faced significant challenges with manual processes, updates in multiple systems and inconsistent data. This case study explores how our expert team helped our customer overcome these challenges and achieve remarkable results.

Challenges

Operational inefficiencies and Data Inconsistencies

The Retail Company grapples with operational inefficiencies due to an extensive reliance on manual processes. The challenge further intensifies with the need to update multiple systems independently, leading to a lack of synchronization and consistency in data. This fragmented approach hinders seamless operations and accurate decision-making.

What N2 Lab Proposed?

To address these challenges, The Retail Company partnered with our expert team to implement the following solutions:

EDI Integration

Our expert team successfully enhanced the business processes of the Retail Technology company through the seamless EDI integration solution, connecting SPS Commerce, NetSuite and trading partners. We delivered a complete and complex suite of integration comprising SOAP web services, EDI 850 for sales order, ASN 856 for sales order item fulfilment, EDI and 810 for invoices. As a result, the order-to-cash process is optimized and streamlined into NetSuite ERP.
